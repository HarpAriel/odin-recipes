Skillet Lasagna

Have lasagna on the table in just 30 minutes with this simple skillet lasagna recipe.

Lasagna is delicious, but for most of us it's too time consuming for a weekday meal at the end of a busy day. With this version cooked on the stove top, you can have your lasanga - and eat it too!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 10 lasagna noodles, broken into 2 inch pieces
  • 28oz can diced tomatoes
  • 8oz tomato sauce
  • 8 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and saute the onions in the olive oil until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. Add the ground beef, and cook, breaking up chunks until the meat in no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle lasagna noodles evenly over the meat. Top with diced tomatoes and tomato sauce. Cover with a lid and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer. Simmer for about 20 minutes, until noodles are tender and most of the liquid is absorbed. (The mixture will look watery after 15 minutes. If it looks dry, add 1/4 cup water.)
  4. Remove from the heat. Stir in parmesan cheese, and season with salt and pepper. Top with large spoonfuls of ricotta. Cover and let sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve topped with fresh basil and grated parmesan.
